Social Aspects of Freshman Year

Freshman year is more than just getting good grades. Here are some important tips on how to survive freshman socially...

  • Find a group of friends that lets you be yourself.
  • Be a nice to everyone.
  • Be social with classmates.
  • Manage your homework and studying so you can hang out with friends.
  • Participating in sports and clubs is a great way to meet new people.
