Mindfulness

Applying one's attention; directing the mind; maintaining a moment-by-moment awareness of our thoughts, feelings, bodily sensations, surrounding environment with an inner attitude of 'allow'; without evaluation or judgement.

Mindful Daily Actions: All of our senses can be used to practice mindfulness any time and anywhere; thus, 'practices' can be as varied as what we can see, hear, taste, smell, feel and think!

  • illuminating the action(s) you already do

  • mindful eating

  • slowing down in your daily habits

  • being conscience of your breathing
