Athletics

Padua Academy offers a variety of different sports at all times of the year. One thing to remember as a freshman: although you might want to tryout for every sport and do everything, that is not necessarily a good idea. While it is great to be involved in sports, school work should always come first; because freshmen year requires a lot of work. Even if you've always done a lot of sports in grade school/middle school, it gets even harder to juggle both sports and academics once you get to highschool.
