Required Service Hours

Each grade has a required amount of hours that must be completed by a certain date.

By Mid February:
  1. Freshmen: At least 7.5 hours must be completed
  2. Sophomore: Hours are optional, but all students will go to Camden for a day of service
  3. Juniors: At least 10 hours must be completed
  4. Seniors: At least 10 hours completed

By the End of the Year:
  1. Freshmen: At least 15 hours must be completed
  2. Sophomores: Hours are optional, but all students will go to Camden for a day of service
  3. Juniors: At least 20 hours of service must be completed
  4. Seniors: At least 20 hours of service must be completed
