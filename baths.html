Baths

Here are the steps to prepare the perfect bath:

  1. Prepare: Make sure your bathroom is clean and warm, so that you feel comfortable when you bathe
  2. Plan a themed bath: Lavender based products are perfect to use because they induce sleep or adding aLush Bath Bombis perfect! They moisturize your skin while making a rainbow of beautiful colors in your tub!
  3. Make sure your bath isn't too hot or to cold: A tub too hot may cause dizziness, and a tub too cold is just uncomfortable!
  4. Food: Make your favorite or beverage to have within reach while bathing.
  5. Atmosphere: Candles are relaxing, turning off the lights is less strain for the eyes, and candles (especially scented ones) are wonderful for light and relaxation.
  6. Music: Put on the perfect playlist. Check out someCalming Music
  7. Bring something to do: Whether it's reading a book or playing a fun game, pick something relaxing to do during your bathing time.
  8. Enjoy your warm tub in a serene environment!
