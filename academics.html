Academics

Academics is an essential part of Freshman year. It is the reason you go to school. Padua Academy is not just a school, it is a place that challenges you and helps you achieve goals you would never imagine achieving. Padua's academics are challenging in all aspects. They offer college preparatory, honors, and advanced placement classes. The teachers and staff challenge and encourage you to work to the best of your ability each day.
